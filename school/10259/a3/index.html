Other Information

  • One of the most popular hobbyist IoT devices, the Raspberry Pi has many, many communities dedicated to all aspects of programming and using them.
  • There are a wide variety of Pi's, from the extreamly low-priced Pi Pico, to the almost-a-desktop capable Pi 4B.
  • The Raspberry Pi, like other computers, is suffering from availability issues due to the global chip shortage.
  • While some unscrupulous vendors are taking advantage of this chip shortage and charging outrageous amounts for their Raspberry Pis, most reputable vendors have kept price increases to a minimum (I've noticed about 10-15% price increase year-over-year on average from some vendors.)
  • The Raspberry Pi 4B initially had a clock speed of 1.5GHz, but the new 64-bit Raspberry Pi OS Bullseye ups the clock speed to 1.8GHz.
