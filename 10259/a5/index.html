Will's Helicopter Game Rules

You are flying through a path. You must avoid the walls to score points. The harder the path is (the narrower the gap to fly through and the faster the game gets) the more points you get.

The game lasts 3 minutes. Try to get the high score!

To control the helicopter, use the mouse in the black area of the screen. Move the mouse up and down to move the helicopter.
